About Lion's Den Gym

Founded in 1981, the Lion's Den is a key club that is open 24/7/365 (except Saturday from 7:30-9:00am when the facility is closed for maintenance and cleaning). We are conveniently located off highway 72 in Athens at 212 Commercial Drive.

The gym was founded by Steve McGlocklin and Jim Swanner in 1981. It was originally in Steve's in-laws' basement and has since moved into a facility with over 10,000 square feet of space.

Make sure to like us on Facebook for the latest news!
